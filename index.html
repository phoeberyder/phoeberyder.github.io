About Me

I am a second-year PhD student at Jodrell Bank Centre for Astrophysics at the University of Manchester, where my research focuses on developing techniques for bistatic radar observations to support space situational awareness. My background includes a Master’s by Research on Galactic magnetic fields and Faraday rotation, and I also contribute to the Jodcast, the UK’s longest-running astronomy podcast.
